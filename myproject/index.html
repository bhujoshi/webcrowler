FAQs
Bookings:

Q: What if the truck doesn't show up?
A: In case the vehicle you booked doesn't show up, we will do arrangement of other truck.   

Q: What if the truck shows up late?
A: We try our best to ensure our partners reach our customers on time. but in case of delays, do call us and we will help you out by either providing an alternate vehicle.  

Payment:

Q: How to do payment?
A: By cash to driver.

Q:Can we use credit/debit card?
A: As of now no, in future you may.  

Cancellations:

Q: Can I cancel my booking?
A: Yes, you can but it should be before driver starts from his location, if driver in your place you have to pay the driver base fair.  

Q: How do I cancel my booking?
A: By calling our call center number.
